## 📝 Problem Statement

Given the **head** of a linked list, rotate the list to the right by `k` places.

---

## 💡 Examples

### **Example 1**
![Example 1 Visual](https://assets.leetcode.com/uploads/2020/11/13/rotate1.jpg)
> **Input:** `head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 2`  
> **Output:** `[4,5,1,2,3]`

### **Example 2**
![Example 2 Visual](https://assets.leetcode.com/uploads/2020/11/13/roate2.jpg)
> **Input:** `head = [0,1,2], k = 4`  
> **Output:** `[2,0,1]`

---

## ⚙️ Constraints
- The number of nodes in the list is in the range `[0, 500]`.
- `-100 <= Node.val <= 100`
- `0 <= k <= 2 * 10^9`
